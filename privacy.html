Privacy and data processing.

This site sets no cookies. Anonymous, cookieless usage statistics run on PostHog EU. If you contact NordSym, we process the information you send.

What we process

Correspondence, contact details and business context you choose to share with us. When you book a review, NordSym uses the submitted name, email, notes, date and time to create and hold the meeting.

Analytics

NordSym uses PostHog EU in cookieless server-hash mode for anonymous site usage statistics. PostHog groups same-day activity with a privacy-preserving hash that rotates daily. On paid qualification pages, NordSym also processes categorical business context selected by the visitor: company-size range, monthly job-volume range, bottleneck category, system-count range and timing. Allowlisted campaign identifiers may be included. These answers contain no name, email address or free text. The site does not use cookies, local storage, session storage, session replay, or named visitor identification.

Booking data

The booking form posts to NordSym's n8n booking workflow, which creates the meeting workflow and related calendar handling. Booking data is used only to coordinate the review and respond to the request.

Client data handling

Client data is handled under written mandate. Client data never crosses client boundaries. Agents operate only inside approved surfaces, scopes and approval paths.

Legal basis

We process correspondence under legitimate interest and contract performance when work is discussed or delivered.

Retention and rights

You can ask for access, correction or deletion by emailing NordSym at contact@nordsym.com.

Terms

Engagement terms are agreed in writing before work starts. Client systems, channels and data remain client-owned unless a written agreement says otherwise.
