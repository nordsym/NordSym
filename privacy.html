NordSym AB · Privacy

Privacy and data processing.

This site sets no cookies and runs no trackers. If you contact NordSym, we process the information you send.

What we process

Correspondence, contact details and business context you choose to share with us. The public site does not create accounts or track visitors.

Legal basis

We process correspondence under legitimate interest and contract performance when work is discussed or delivered.

Client data handling

Client data is handled under written mandate. Client data never crosses client boundaries. Agents operate only inside approved surfaces, scopes and approval paths.

Retention and rights

You can ask for access, correction or deletion by emailing Gustav Hemmingsson at gustav@nordsym.com.

Analytics

NordSym uses PostHog EU in cookieless mode for anonymous site usage statistics. It does not use cookies, local storage, session storage, session replay, or visitor identification on this site.

Booking data

When you book a review, NordSym uses the submitted name, email, notes, date and time only to create and hold the meeting.
