Systems boundary

The systems NordSym deliberately makes visible.

NordSym runs on architecture it built. Some of it is public. Most of it is not. This page lists what is public enough for humans and agents to inspect without crossing a client boundary.

Boundary rule

This is not a product museum. It is a public map of the products, infrastructure, and proof layers that explain how NordSym builds and operates agent systems. Private client agreement surfaces, internal runtime maps, and client-specific agents stay out of public discovery files.

The operating room stays internal.

NordSym itself runs on Mission Control, an internal operating surface for approvals, records, and agent state. It is not public.

Agent Atlas is the internal canon for agents, runtimes, schedules, and stop controls. It explains the architecture. It is not a public product.
