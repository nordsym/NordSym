Questions worth asking.

How do agents stay governed?

At the boundary, before an action reaches your stack: written scope, approval gates, a stop control and a record of the work.

Where does our data live?

In your systems. We connect with scoped access and document every data path before an agent operates. Client boundaries are hard-separated.

Can we stop, and who owns what was built?

One control halts an operation. You own your systems, channels and data. On exit, the layer is paused, handed over or removed per the agreed scope.

What do you actually operate?

Recurring business work with real cadence: revenue, publishing, support, finance, reporting and whatever your stack and mandate justify.
