NS-01  /  General Arrangement

The operating layer between your stack and the agents that run it.

NordSym is a forward-deployed engineering firm. We design, build, and operate the layer that lets AI agents safely run real business operations inside the stack you already have.

Your stack stays yours. We integrate into your stack, give agents context and a mandate, meaning a written scope of what they may do, govern at the boundary, and run the work continuously.
